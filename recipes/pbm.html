Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

For the Paneer

  • 250 grams paneer, cubed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts, soaked and blended to a paste
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

Preparing the Paneer Butter Masala

  1. Heat 1 tbsp butter and 1 tbsp oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another 2-3 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add the pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  5. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add the cashew nut paste and cook for 5-7 minutes until the gravy thickens.
  7. Add the cubed paneer and gently mix to coat the paneer with the gravy. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. Add fresh cream and kasuri methi. Mix well and simmer for 2-3 minutes.

Serving

  1. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
